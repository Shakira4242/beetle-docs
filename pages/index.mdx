# Overview
 
This is an overview on the Beetle sales AI.

## What is Beetle?

A **simple** and **intuitive** bot that interacts with your website to guide users through your website. It's the equivalent of a having a sales assitant co-browsing your website with your users.

## What can Beetle do?

It can do a lot of things, but here are some of the most important ones:
1. **Guide users** - Beetle can guide users through your website and help them find what they are looking for.
2. **Answer questions** - Beetle can answer questions about your products and services.
3. **Collect leads** - Beetle can collect leads and send them to your CRM.
